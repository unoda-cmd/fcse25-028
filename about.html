Our History & Mandate

Air Botswana is the state-owned national flag carrier airline of Botswana, headquartered directly in Gaborone.

Formed originally in 1988, the national carrier operates specialized domestic and regional scheduled flights. We remain heavily committed to driving socio-economic growth frameworks across Botswana by reinforcing critical tourist corridors and regional economic zones.
